A coupon allows a customer to receive a discount by applying a code to a cart. A coupon can be shared across customers or unique per customer and can apply to a specific product or products, an entire order or shipping charges.

A cross sell is a group of secondary offers presented to a customer in a cart, with or without a discount, based on the items that have already been added to the cart. A cross sell is presented as related items that the user may be interested in, based on the items that are already in their cart.

Create Cross Sell

An up sell is a group of replacement offers presented to a customer in a cart, with or without a discount, based on the items that have already been added to the cart. An up sell is presented as an "upgrade" to an item the user has already added to their cart. When a user accepts an up sell offer, the item that generated the up sell is removed and the up sell item is added to the cart.
